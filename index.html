Our Specialty

The art of
balayage.

“Balayage” — French for “to sweep.”

Many clients come in looking to change their look without the harsh commitment of a one-process color or traditional highlight. Balayage is one of the most versatile highlighting techniques — and it's a look we happen to specialize in creating.

This method opens doors to specialty colors with a natural, dimensional finish that grows out beautifully.
