Choosing a stylist.

Please specify if you prefer the Creative Director, a Master Stylist, or a Junior Stylist when you book your appointment. Each level varies in price based on texture, length, volume, time, and experience. Urban Halo does not quote over the phone — we recommend an in-person consultation with one of our experienced artists. Prices reflect basic pricing and your costs may vary; please ask your stylist before service begins.

Cancellation policy.

Appointments require a credit card guarantee. Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled time. Kindly provide at least 48 hours notice for changes or cancellations — missed appointments and same-day cancellations may be subject to a fee of 50% of the booked service. Arrivals 15 minutes late may result in a loss of reserved time; 30 minutes late is considered a missed appointment. We accept cash, Visa, MasterCard, AmEx, and Urban Halo gift certificates. An ATM is available inside the salon.
