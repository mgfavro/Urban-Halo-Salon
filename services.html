Choosing a stylist.

Please specify if you prefer the Creative Director, a Master Stylist, or a Junior Stylist when you book. Each level varies in price based on texture, length, volume, time, and experience. Urban Halo does not quote over the phone — we recommend an in-person consultation. Prices are a starting point and may vary; please confirm with your stylist before service begins.

Cancellation policy.

Appointments require a credit card guarantee. Please arrive 15 minutes early. Kindly provide 48 hours notice for changes or cancellations — missed appointments and same-day cancellations may be charged 50% of the booked service. Arrivals 15 minutes late may forfeit reserved time; 30 minutes late is a missed appointment. We accept cash, Visa, MasterCard, AmEx, and Urban Halo gift certificates. An ATM is available inside the salon.
